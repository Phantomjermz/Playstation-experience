The PS5

The PS5's powerful hardware enables high-quality graphics, fast load times, and smooth gameplay. The PS5 has a growing library of exclusive games, including Demon's Souls, Spider-Man: Miles Morales, and Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart, which offer unique and compelling gaming experiences. The ability to play most PS4 games on the PS5 is a great feature for those who want to continue playing their favorite titles from the previous generation.
